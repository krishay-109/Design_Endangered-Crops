Endangered Crops

Endangered Crops

Red Sandalwood

Red sandalwood is highly valued for its aromatic heartwood, often used in incense and traditional medicine. Its demand has led to significant over-exploitation.

Musli

Musli, a medicinal herb known for its aphrodisiac properties, is facing extinction due to habitat loss and overharvesting.

Asam Catkin

The Asam catkin is a unique flowering plant that plays a vital role in local ecosystems but is threatened by agricultural expansion.

Ebony Tree

Ebony trees are prized for their dark, dense wood used in fine furniture. Overexploitation and deforestation have led to its endangered status.
